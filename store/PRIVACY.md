# Privacy Policy — Vim Keybindings for Pluto

_Last updated: 2026-04-12_

This extension does not collect, transmit, or share any personal or user data.

## What the extension does

- Adds Vim-style keybindings to CodeMirror editors on pages served from `http://localhost:*`, `http://127.0.0.1:*`, or `https://localhost:*` (i.e., local Pluto notebook servers).
- Stores a single on/off flag in `chrome.storage.local` so the setting persists across browser sessions.

## What it does **not** do

- It does not read, transmit, or store the content of your notebooks or cells.
- It does not make any network requests.
- It does not contact any third-party server or analytics service.
- It does not collect identifiers, usage statistics, or any other data.
- It does not execute any remotely hosted code. All scripts ship inside the extension package.

## Contact

For questions, open an issue at https://github.com/simoneSantoni/vim-kb-for-pluto/issues.
